About

Hello! I am a Software Engineer based in Connecticut who enjoys working with modern programming technologies and is passionate about building innovative products in fast-paced, collaborative environments. I graduated from Dickinson College in 2016 with B.S. in Computer Science. I have 5 years of professional software engineering experience.

I have strong experience in various Computer Science and Software Engineering related subjects including but not limited to: Object-Oriented Programming, Functional Programming, Databases, Networks, Full-stack Web Development, Web Services, Microservices, Source Control, Agile Methodologies, and DevOps.

I am a "T-shaped" developer that specializes with programming technologies such as Java, Spring Boot, JavaScript, TypeScript, Angular, NestJS, Node.js, SQL, AWS, GraphQL, REST, and Git, but I can adapt quickly to work with whatever toolset is needed for the job.
